The book milk and honey is a collection of poetry and prose divided into four thematic sections: "the hurting," "the loving," "the breaking," and "the healing." " The poems offer a candid and vulnerable exploration of topics like survival, abuse, violence, love, loss, and femininity. Kaur employs a minimalist style using lowercase letters and simple language, often accompanied by line art illustrations. The collection is essentially a journey charting the recovery and empowerment found after experiencing pain.
